About This Website

The basic website displays data from the New York Department of Health and Mental Hygiene to show the leading causes of death by race and gender in the New York metropolitan area. Data is available since 2007 and ICD-10 CM codes are displayed (these are administrative codes used for insurance billing and quality assurance metrics). The data shows that for every day we combat illness and injury, the fact we survive at all is a miracle.

But mostly it is just an excuse to try out Bootstrap, EJS, React, and server-to-server API call interactions.
